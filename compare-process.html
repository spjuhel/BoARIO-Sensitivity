Comparison process¶

How we aggregate results to have them comparable

We run the simulation with each MRIOT typology. But in order to compare readable results we aggregate to a select set of regions and sectors. The reasoning behind the aggregation as well as the mapping for each MRIOT is presented in the supplementary materials.

Region aggregation¶

We aggregate/select to DEU, FRA, CHN, USA and a rest of the world ROW region.

Sector aggregation¶

We aggregate the to 6 sectors, namely:

  • Agriculture and Other

  • Construction

  • Energy and Utilities and Mining

  • Manufacture

  • Sales, Transports and Services

  • Others
